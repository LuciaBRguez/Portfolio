About me

Hey! My name is Lucía and I'm a web developer with passion for front end development and design.

I've studied a degree in Telecommunications Engineering and a master degree in Web Egineering. I aspire toward a career that will allow me to channel my creativity through crafting beatiful software and engaging experiences.

When I'm not on the computer I enjoy doing sports, yoga and travelling. I like experiencing new environments and cultures, and I thus have been working in UK as an aupair and have done an Erasmus in Italy.

I am a native Spanish and Galician speaker. I also have a medium-high level in English and a basic level in Italian.
